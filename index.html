Domestic Cat 🐈

Cats love cat nip, laser pointers, eating grass, scratching the couch, sunbathing, sleeping on a human's lap, and shedding everywhere.

  • Scientific Name: Felidae
  • Average Length: 46 cm (18 in)
  • Average Lifespan: 12–15 years
  • Habitat: All continents except Antarctica

Cats are furry, lovable creatures. My cats are kept indoors and are valued for their companionship. They are flexible, have quick reflexes, and sharp teeth and claws. When a cat meows or purrs they are happy. When a cat hisses or growls they are uncomfortable. (Source: Wikipedia: Cats)
